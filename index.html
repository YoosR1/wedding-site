Our Love Story

First Meet

Spring 2014

We met in the last half of 8th grade, growing closer over video games! We stayed connected purely because Jorge left his phone number in Victoria's yearbook and she reached out to finally play TF2 over the summer. We stayed closely connected ever since!

Officially Together

July 6, 2015

After multiple nights of endless gaming and aimless chatting, we finally decided to open up about our little secrets: we liked each other! Neither of us asked the other out, it was just agreed upon that we were officially dating. This started our relationship off on a strong footing, and we are still best friends and close partners today! Not everyone can say they are marrying their best friend.

First Date

July 6, 2016

Although we spent many weekends hanging out together and many late nights talking on the phone until we fell asleep, our first year anniversary was also our first date! We visited Bayside Marketplace and took a sunset cruise through Biscayne Bay.

Proposal

December 19, 2021

During a Christmas celebration, Jorge hid the ring box at the bottom of Victoria's Secret Santa gift for her to find! He dropped to one knee as she pulled out the box, quickly realizing what was happening and tearing up! He proposed with a beautiful Alexandrite gem and rose gold ring in a cute brown box while we were surrounded by friends.

Moving in Together

January 8, 2022

We started off our engagement by moving in to Orlando together. Living together always brings new challenges, and we continue to push forward through what life gives us together!
